APFC Panels

Automatic Power Factor Control (APFC) Panels are critical in modern electrical systems, designed to improve Power Factor (PF) — the ratio of real power (kW) to apparent power (kVA) — which indicates how efficiently power is being used. A low Power Factor can result in increased electricity costs, inefficiencies, and stress on electrical infrastructure, making APFC panels essential for maintaining optimal electrical efficiency.Additionally, low Power Factor increases the load on equipment such as transformers, cables, and switchgear
